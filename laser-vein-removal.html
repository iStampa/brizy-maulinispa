Laser Vein Removal

Laser technology is versatile and used in a plethora of medical and cosmetic procedures. Because it is highly effective, a lot of doctors use laser technology to deliver effective and long-term results for a variety of conditions. One place laser technology is effective is in vein treatment.

Using laser technology, you can effectively reduce and even eliminate the appearance of unsightly veins and improve your blood circulation.

What Does Laser Vein Removal do?

Laser vein treatment uses a laser to target veins beneath the surface of the skin. After heating, the vessel walls collapse and seal shut. The sealed vein is then broken down and absorbed by the body. While it is not guaranteed to treat every vessel, a majority of patients experience significant improvement several weeks after treatment.

FAQS

How does Laser Vein Removal work?

Using laser technology, you can effectively reduce and even eliminate the appearance of unsightly veins and improve your blood circulation. The most popular treatment method for unsightly veins is endovascular laser ablation (EVLA).

Will It Hurt?

A brief pinching sensation is very common as the laser device releases pulses of energy. Your first spider vein treatment may be slightly more uncomfortable than any additional treatments, and you can always request a topical anesthetic to maximize your comfort.

What about Recovery and Results?

Improving the appearance of spider veins is a gradual process. Each treatment causes the unwanted veins to begin to slowly dissolve, and maximum improvement typically wont be seen for several weeks, or even months. Right after treatment, it is not uncommon for veins to appear darker and more pronounced before beginning to fade

How Long Will My Results Last?

Treated veins do not grow back, but you should keep in mind that over time new visible veins are likely to develop that will need to be treated to maintain your youthful-looking skin. Many of our patients choose laser hair removal after their vein treatment to complete their new look.
