FAQS

Is Botox safe?

While many read stories of botched Botox treatments, many do not realize that Botox has been used safely and successfully for over 30 years. Botox is incredibly safe when administered by the skilled and licensed providers of Maulini Esthetics Body & Skin Spa.


Does a Botox treatment hurt?

BOTOX is described to be virtually painless. Some may say that Botox is mildly uncomfortable upon injection, however most patients report that they don’t even feel the treatment being administered. Some areas tend to be more sensitive than others.


How long will the results last?

The effects of Botox generally last 3 to 4 monthis. As the Botox is absorbed, muscle action gradually returns and with it the lines and wrinkles caused by those muscles.

Who can be treated with Botox?

Botox Cosmetic is suitable for men and women ages 20-60+

Who should not get Botox Treatments?

Botox is NOT suitable for those who have an infection at the proposed injection site(s) and in individuals with known hypersensitivity to any botulinum toxin preparation or to any of the components in the formulation. Caution should be used when Botox is intended to be used on areas where there is inflammation present, or there is excessive weakness or atrophy of the muscles to be targeted

When will I see results from Botox?

Results from a Botox treatment typically develop 14 days post treatment. In some rare cases, results can onset as soon as 5 days post treatment

Will my results look natural?

Yes, because we at Maulini Esthetics Body & Skin Spa believe in the conservative approach, a first time Botox treatment will generally include up to typical dosing for the respective treated areas. A follow appointment 2 weeks after treatment may be required to deliver more Botox should further correction be desired. Follow up treatments do require additional purchases and are not included as part of the initial treatment.
