Dermal Filler

Dermal filler injections provide an easy way to reduce the appearance of wrinkles, smooth the skin and give a more youthful appearance. The result of a single treatment can be seen instantly and can last for around 6 to 9 months. Dermal filler injections can give a natural looking result that does not affect facial expression.

 

Dermal fillers can be used to smooth away facial lines and wrinkles, create fuller lips and shape facial contours, such as cheeks and chin, and revitalize the skin.

What Does Dermal Filler Do?

Dermal fillers are used for facial aesthetic treatments and are injected into the skin. They are designed to effectively reduce the appearance of unwanted wrinkles, contour and create volume, and to revitalize the skin. Dermal fillers are based on Hyaluronic acid which is a substance that naturally exists in your body.


 Dermal fillers are a soft injectable gel consisting of stabilized, non-animal hyaluronic acid. The gel is tissue-friendly and closely resembles the hyaluronic acid that exists naturally in the body. It is long-lasting but not permanent.

FAQS

Are Dermal Filler permanent?

Botox and fillers are temporary injections that slowly fade over time. It is recommended to treat with Botox every 3 to 6 months. Injectable fillers such as Juvederm, Restylane, Belotero typically last up to 6 months. Voluma and Volbella may last up to 2 years. Enhancements and touchups are required to upkeep results with injectable fillers

Are there any side effects of Dermal Filler?

Main side effects of Injectable fillers include itching, rash, red itchy welts, wheezing, asthma symptoms, or dizziness or feeling faint. This is not intended to be the full list of side effects.

Do facial injections hurt?

If you are very sensitive, you may opt for a numbing cream to be applied, to which no sensation may be experienced upon injecting. For fillers such as cheeks and jawline, you may feel a slight amount of tension where injected. Most people typically receive treatment with no numbing cream as they describe the treatments to have little to no pain.

How long does a treatment take?

Treatments typically take about 15-45 minutes or more depending on the area(s) you wish to have treated

How soon will I see results?

Once treated, you may return to work, or travel. With injectable fillers, results are typically seen instantly

What kind of results will I see?

With fillers you can reduce almost any fold, wrinkle or crease! Define your jawline, build volume in the cheeks, and increase the dimensions of your facial structure, increase or correct your lip structure and much more.

Who should not get Dermal Filler?

Those who are pregnant or breastfeeding. Those with an infection at or near desired treatment site. Those who are planning on working out same day of the appointment. Those who were drinking the night before, or will plan to drink same day of your appointment (may make bruising more apparent). Other medical considerations will be covered during your free consultation
